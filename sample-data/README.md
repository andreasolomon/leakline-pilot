# LeakLine sample CSVs

These files model **Ascend Growth Partners**, a fake high-ticket coaching/education company selling $4.5k–$14k programmes.

Upload the files from the **Connect or Import Data** screen in this order:

1. `leads.csv`
2. `appointments.csv`
3. `deals.csv`
4. `payments.csv`
5. `closers.csv`

The sample intentionally includes:

- opted-in leads who never booked
- booked calls that did not attend
- won deals with deposits and payment plans
- failed, overdue and unpaid balances
- refunds, chargebacks and written-off revenue
- stale open opportunities with no next action
- closer performance variance

Expected imported demo totals:

- 30 leads
- 18 booked calls
- 8 attended calls
- 15 deals
- 17 payment rows
- 5 closers
- 6 leak alerts
